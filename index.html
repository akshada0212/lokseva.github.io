About Us

A Hustling community, ready to serve for the better!

LokSeva is a Community and Social Platform founded by Imperium Official in the year 2018. With over 5000+ volunteers all over top tier cities like Pune, Mumbai, Banglore etc. LokSeva aims to provide community services to the underpriviledged and weaker sections of the society. LokSeva's Free Education Community aims at providing education to underpriviledged kids of the weaker sections. Along with the Education Community, there are various projects that LokSeva undertakes, like the Free Meal Schemes, Woman Empowerment Projects, Blood Donation Camps, Fundraisers for the underpriviledged etc.
