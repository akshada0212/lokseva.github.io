About the Free Education Committee

The Free Education Committee of LokSeva is dedicated to bridging educational gaps by offering free resources, tutoring, and mentorship to children in underserved communities. Our goal is to make education accessible to all, empowering children to unlock their full potential and secure brighter futures.

With a dedicated team of volunteers, we organize classes, distribute educational materials, and provide personalized support to students who lack access to essential learning opportunities. We believe that education is a powerful tool to bring positive change to society, and we welcome passionate individuals to join our mission.
