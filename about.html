About Us

A Hustling community, ready to serve for the better!

LokSeva is a Community and Social Platform founded by Imperium Official in the year 2018. With over 5000+ volunteers all over top tier cities like Pune, Mumbai, Banglore etc. LokSeva aims to provide community services to the underpriviledged and weaker sections of the society. LokSeva's Free Education Community aims at providing education to underpriviledged kids of the weaker sections. Along with the Education Community, there are various projects that LokSeva undertakes, like the Free Meal Schemes, Woman Empowerment Projects, Blood Donation Camps, Fundraisers for the underpriviledged etc.

LokSeva has made a significant impact by fostering collaboration among volunteers, local organizations, and community leaders. Through its diverse initiatives, LokSeva not only addresses immediate needs but also empowers individuals to build sustainable futures. The platform encourages community engagement, providing training and resources to volunteers, ensuring that they can effectively contribute to the causes they are passionate about.

In addition to education and meal programs, LokSeva organizes health camps that offer free medical check-ups and essential health services, further supporting the well-being of marginalized communities. The organization also emphasizes environmental sustainability through awareness campaigns and tree-planting drives, promoting a healthier planet for future generations. By creating a network of compassionate individuals dedicated to service, LokSeva strives to inspire positive change and uplift the communities it serves.
